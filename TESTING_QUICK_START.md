
# GlobalTalk Testing Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the GlobalTalk beta testing program! This guide will help you get started quickly.

## Installation

### Android

1. **Download the APK**
   - Click the download link provided in your invitation email
   - Or scan the QR code with your camera app

2. **Enable Installation from Unknown Sources**
   - Go to Settings ‚Üí Security
   - Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" or "Unknown Sources"
   - (Location may vary by device)

3. **Install the App**
   - Open the downloaded APK file
   - Tap "Install"
   - Wait for installation to complete
   - Tap "Open" to launch

### iOS

1. **Install TestFlight**
   - Download TestFlight from the App Store
   - It's free and required for beta testing

2. **Accept Invitation**
   - Open the invitation email on your iOS device
   - Tap "View in TestFlight"
   - Tap "Accept" to join the beta

3. **Install GlobalTalk**
   - Tap "Install" in TestFlight
   - Wait for download to complete
   - Tap "Open" to launch

## First Time Setup

1. **Open GlobalTalk**
   - Launch the app from your home screen

2. **Create Account**
   - Enter a username (must be unique)
   - Enter your display name
   - Select your preferred language

3. **Grant Permissions**
   - Allow notifications (recommended)
   - Allow camera access (for photos)
   - Allow microphone access (for voice messages)

## How to Report Bugs

### In-App Reporting (Recommended)

1. Go to Profile tab
2. Tap "Send Feedback"
3. Select "Report a Bug"
4. Fill in the details:
   - Title: Brief description
   - Description: Detailed steps to reproduce
   - Email: (optional) for follow-up
5. Tap "Submit Feedback"

### Bug Report Template

If reporting via email, use this template:

```
Subject: [Bug] Brief description

Device: [Your device model]
OS Version: [Android/iOS version]
App Version: [Check in Profile ‚Üí About]

Issue:
[Describe what went wrong]

Steps to Reproduce:
1. [First step]
2. [Second step]
3. [Third step]

Expected Result:
[What should have happened]

Actual Result:
[What actually happened]

Screenshots:
[Attach if available]

Additional Notes:
[Any other relevant information]
```

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often should I test?**
A: Use the app regularly, as you would any messaging app. The more you use it, the more issues you'll find!

**Q: What if I find a critical bug?**
A: Report it immediately through the in-app feedback form and mark it as urgent.

**Q: Can I invite friends to test?**
A: Not yet, but we'll open up testing to more users soon!

**Q: Will my data be saved?**
A: Test data may be reset between major updates. Don't use for important conversations yet!

**Q: How long is the testing period?**
A: We'll test for 2-4 weeks before public launch, depending on feedback.

**Q: What happens after testing?**
A: You'll get early access to the production app and all your feedback will help shape the final product!
